The Gridlock Problem

Our cities are growing, but our traffic infrastructure is stuck in the past. Inefficient, static traffic signals lead to daily congestion, wasted fuel, increased pollution, and critical delays for emergency services.

Voice-Enabled Guidance

The system captures traffic signal data and delivers clear, audible commands to drivers, such as "Red light ahead, please stop." This feature enhances driver awareness and safety, particularly for visually impaired individuals.

Real-Time Traffic Analysis

By analyzing traffic flow in real-time, the system dynamically adjusts signal timings. Lanes with heavy traffic are given longer green lights, minimizing bottlenecks and significantly reducing overall wait times across junctions.

Emergency Priority System

Using sound recognition, the system detects approaching emergency vehicle sirens. It automatically turns the signal green for their path while holding other traffic & also announces emergency vehicle ahead please wait, clearing the way and drastically reducing response times.
